Fans celebrate after hearing the unofficial news that plans for a new European Super League have been cancelled, during protests by Chelsea fans outside Stamford Bridge stadium in London, against Chelsea's decision to be included amongst the clubs attempting to form a new European Super League, Tuesday, April 20, 2021.
